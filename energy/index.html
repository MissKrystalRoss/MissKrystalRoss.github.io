About me

Greetings,

I'm Krystal Ross and this is my webpage. Notice that my name is spelled with the letter 'K' and not the letter 'C'. I mention this because when your name sounds just like a noun, there is going to be confusion in some people's minds.

There is something about crystals many people don't know. Crystals aren't formed in an instant, crystals grow over time. Each molecule must fit perfectly in its position or the crystal doesn't form.

Carbon usually exists as graphite but a carbon crystal is a diamond. So that's my name. My name means time, order, strength and beauty. Crystals are perfection and I'm Krystal.....but with a 'K'.
